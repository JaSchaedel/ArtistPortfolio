About Me

Hello! Iâ€™m J. Schaedel, an artist inspired by the essence of beauty and imagination mixed with abstract visions of a colorful world. I enjoy capturing the beauty of nature and mixing it with the flow and splashes of traditional and digital watercolors, sketches, and photography. My work is characterized by a love for abstract and minimalistic styles, with a strong focus on nature and animals.

Through my art, I aim to capture the essence of the natural world and present it in my unique artistic perspective. Mixing chaos and beauty in splashes, splatters, and drips of color. I hope to evoke a sense of wonder and connection for my viewers.

As a self-taught artist hailing from a creative family, I've been immersed in the world of art for most of my life. This lifelong passion has shaped my unique artistic voice and continues to drive my exploration and growth. Every day presents new opportunities to learn and grow, and I am constantly evolving my style and techniques.

Thank you for visiting my site. I hope my artwork brings you as much joy and inspiration as I find in creating it.
